İAFL's History

İstanbul Atatürk Highschool of Science found in 1982 as "Turkey's second highschool of science" upon Kenan Evren's request. Although found in 1982, this schools history goes way back to Ottoman Empire. In 17th Century Sultan Murad V used this schools campus area as a hunting lodge. In between 1967 and 1980 building of highschool was used as Marmara University Atatürk Faculty of education . After that building and land was used as garrison command in 1980 Turkish coup d'état Between 2017 and 2019, main building reconstructed. And the dorm that was in school's main building transported to a new building in campus.

AFLİM

AFLİM is alumni association of Atatürk Highschool of Science. With helpful and involving graduates, this assoc. assists and funds İAFL's main events.
